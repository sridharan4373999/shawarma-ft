Delectable Meals On The Go

At Oho Shawarma, we have created a menu that is suitable for fast-paced individuals. Welcome to Oho Shawarma

Discover Our Story

At Oho Shawarma, we believe in the power of eating a good, flavourful meal. All our products are made using branded ingredients in a strictly hygienic environment. We understand the importance of time in this fast-paced world, which is why we have curated our food for working individuals. Although we have added a twist to the traditional shawarma, by adding the zing to suit Indian customers, we have retained the Middle Eastern flavour in the food.

Oho Shawarma Promises to Deliver a Mouth-Watering Meal Every Time.
